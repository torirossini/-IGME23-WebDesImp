About Me

Hi, I'm Tori Rossini! I'm a game programmer and developer, and I'm always looking to broaden my skillset. I'm particularly skilled scripting in C# and Java, and have a passion for breaking my games in every way a player possibly could, in order to have an extremely well-tested and thoughtful final product. I have the most experience with gameplay programming, but am looking to deepen my understanding of different design principles in games. I'm also extremely interested in tech art.

I'm currently in my second year of college, studying Game Design and Development major at Rochester Institute of Technology. I am also the Social Director for Computer Science House, which is an organization driven by passion for personal projects and learning about as much as possible.
